MyTimetable 2016 UK User Conference

Monday 8 February 2016 – Cardiff University

We are pleased to announce that the first MyTimetable UK User Conference will take place at Cardiff University, Monday 8 February 2016. Please save the date and look forward to a great opportunity to learn more about MyTimetable and to share experiences with your peers.

We look forward to seeing you in Cardiff this February!

Call for sessions

Based on our Dutch User Conference, we have already prepared a basic programme containing the sessions mentioned below. Since the User Conference is aimed at the MyTimetable users, we invite you to send proposals for presentations, workshops, and discussions as well. These can be sessions you want to present or chair yourself, or sessions you would like Eveoh to prepare.

Please submit your proposal to Marco Krikke at marco@eveoh.nl. Marco is also available for any queries you might have.

  • MyTimetable 3.0, overview of new features
  • Distance learning with Pexip, Syllabus Plus and MyTimetable
  • Workshop: MyTimetable roadmap
  • Attendance registration and MyTimetable
  • In-depth: less known features of MyTimetable
  • In-depth: technical application management of MyTimetable
  • Linking MyTimetable with your SIS and Electronic Learning Environment
  • Timetabling data sources
  • Networking session
