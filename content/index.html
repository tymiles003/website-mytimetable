Personalised and up-to-date timetables for students and staff

MyTimetable publishes timetables via Web and mobile phone, and connects to a wide range of calendar applications. Present your users their personal timetable, or let them personalise their timetables in MyTimetable.

Timetabling data is fetched directly from your timetabling application, resulting in timetables that are always up-to-date and accurate.

Integrates with other applications

MyTimetable integrates with a wide range of available calendar applications, and offers push-based integration with Microsoft Office 365, Microsoft Exchange, and Google Apps for Education. Integrations are also available for leading electronic learning environments.

Automatically personalise your timetables by using data from your student information system. Or integrate your timetabling data with portals and apps using our API.
