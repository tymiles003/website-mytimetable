Personalised and up-to-date timetables for students and staff

MyTimetable publishes your timetables via web and mobile phone and connects to numerous calendar applications. Present your users their personal timetable or let them fully personalise their timetable in MyTimetable.

Data is fetched directly from your timetabling application, resulting in timetables that are always up-to-date and accurate.

Learn more about our features

Integrates with other applications

MyTimetable integrates your timetables with a wide range of calendar applications and offers push-based integration with Microsoft Office 365, Microsoft Exchange and Google Apps for Education. Integrations are also available for leading digital learning environments.

Automatically personalise your timetables by using data from your student information system. Or integrate your timetabling data with portals and apps using our API.

Learn more about integrations

Great customer service

Timetables are part of your core services, and your student and staff rely on their availability and correctness. Student satisfaction, timetable availability, and quick incident response and resolution times are all very important.
