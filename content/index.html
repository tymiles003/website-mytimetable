MyTimetable

Up-to-date and personalised timetables,
always & everywhere...

MyTimetable presents your timetables in a clear, intuitive and personalised way. Offer your students and staff members their timetables on the web, on their mobile phones and through your e-learning portal. Already 300,000 students are using MyTimetable on a daily basis.

Timetables, everywhere

Next to offering a modern web interface for your timetables, MyTimetable also provides a mobile web site, an open API, and can be used with your favorite calendar applications.

Always up-to-date

Timetabling data is fetched directly from your timetabling application, such as Scientia Syllabus Plus. This way, timetables are always up-to-date and accurate.

Integration

MyTimetable integrates with your existing student information systems, student portals, and authentication services to provide your users with a seamless experience.

Please contact us for a demonstration.

Features

MyTimetable offers a wide range of features. New features are added all the time, based on customer input and demand.

Personal timetables

All users have their own personal profile, which enables them to fully personalise their timetable. Depending on the wishes of the institution, students and staff members can add and remove courses, modules, study programmes or other timetables.

MyTimetable can also be integrated with your student information system. This way, the courses students are enroled in are automatically added to their profile.

Multiple timetable views

Timetables are shown in a modern web interface, offering both graphical and textual views, to meet the needs of every user.

Students and staff members will be familiar with and appreciate the graphical views, whereas porters and building managers can use the availability view to quickly locate unoccupied locations in a building.

Integration with third-party calendars

We believe users should be free to choose their own calendar application. Using our iCalendar feeds users can link their own calendar to MyTimetable.

Microsoft Outlook, Google Calendar, Zimbra and many other applications support the iCalendar format and are able to keep a users timetable up-to-date continuously.

Mobile ready

A clean and lightweight mobile interface is automatically shown when using a mobile phone. MyTimetable runs in your mobile browser, so no need to install an app.

MyTimetable supports Apple, Android, Windows Phone and Blackberry devices.

Exports in many formats

Users can export their schedule in multiple formats, such as PDF, CSV, iCalendar and xCalendar. This gives users the opportunity to use their timetabling data in various applications (e.g., Microsoft Outlook, Excel, Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and more). The PDF option makes it easy to print your timetable while retaining the great layout of MyTimetable.

Full Scientia Syllabus Plus support

MyTimetable can be directly linked to the Scientia Syllabus Plus Enterprise Reporting Database. All common Syllabus Plus timetabling workflows are supported and MyTimetable can always adapt to your institution's specifics.

Open API

A fully RESTful API is available to reuse timetabling data and user profile data in other applications. Data is returned in JSON or XML formats. The API is secured using the OAuth 2 protocol, putting the end-users in control of their data.

Open software

MyTimetable is licensed under an open-source license (GPLv3), which prevents vendor lock-in and gives you full freedom to alter the software when necessary. Of course Eveoh is always available to provide professional support.

Security

Timetabling data can be fully secured using a role-based access model. For example, staff or draft timetables can be hidden from students when necessary, or students can be locked down to their personal timetable.

On-site or SaaS hosting

MyTimetable can be hosted on your servers in your data center, both on Microsoft Windows and Linux servers. Of course, Eveoh can also provide you with a high quality managed hosting environment, including a service level agreement.
